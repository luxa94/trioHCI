Modify premises

      When you select item from table you can modify in tab "Modify". Previously entered values appears and you can change them by positioning in appropriate cell. Value "Id" is unique and onced, when new premises is made, you have no more possibility of changing. Other values like "Name", "Description", "Price", "Capacity", "Opening date", "Alcohol serving" and check boxes like "Accessible for persons with disabilities", ""Smoking allowed" and "Reservations avaiable" have posibility of changing in every moment. You are allowed to choose type for each premises. Also, you can add new type or browse picture from sysem by pressing on appropriate button.

Click on button "Save" is saving your changes, unlike the click on button "Cancel", which turns you on previous conditions of values. By pressing on button "Delete" you can delete previously selected premises.
